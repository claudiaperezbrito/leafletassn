The Rinconcitos Latinos of Miami

This map was created using Leaflet by Claudia Perez Brito for educational purposes. It is a map of all the "Rinconcitos Latinos" in Miami, FL. It is a chain of restaurants that serve Cuban cuisine. Aside from the restaurants having an inside place where customers can sit and eat their food, most of them have a window where customers can buy Cuban coffee and pastries.
